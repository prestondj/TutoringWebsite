Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

PDJ Tutoring respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use this website, and informs you of your privacy rights under UK law.

For the purposes of the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), PDJ Tutoring is the data controller. The use of the term "we", "us", and "our" in this policy refers to PDJ Tutoring. By using this website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

When you submit an enquiry via the contact form, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your message

How Your Data Is Used & Lawful Basis

We process your personal data under the following lawful bases:

  • Legitimate Interests: To respond to enquiries, answer questions, and provide information regarding our tutoring services.
  • Contractual/Pre-contractual Steps: To arrange lessons, schedule sessions, or manage bookings at your explicit request.

Your information is used solely for these purposes. We do not use your data for unsolicited marketing or sell it to third parties.

Data Storage and International Transfers

Messages submitted through this website are processed securely via Formspree and delivered by email. Because Formspree operates globally, the information you submit may be transferred to, stored, and processed on servers located outside the UK (such as the United States). Formspree ensures compliance with the UK GDPR through robust technical safeguards and standard contractual data protection clauses.

Data is retained only for as long as necessary to handle your enquiry or to manage your ongoing tutoring arrangement.

Cookies

This website does not use tracking or analytical cookies. We only utilize essential, functional cookies if required by our hosting provider or form service to process your message securely and prevent spam.

Your Rights

Under the UK GDPR, you have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. You also have the right to object to processing, request the restriction of processing, or request data portability.

To make a request, please contact us using the details on the contact page. If you are not satisfied with how we handle your data, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please use the contact form on this website.
